// 01 — Image Deduplication
Dupe Remove

Find and remove duplicate photos. Fast, private, runs entirely on your Mac.

If you shoot a lot of photos, you know the problem — hundreds of near-identical shots clogging up your drive. Dupe Remove finds them fast using perceptual hashing, groups them visually, and lets you decide what to keep. Nothing is deleted without your say-so.

  • Detects exact and visually similar duplicates — not just identical files
  • Group review — see duplicates side by side before deleting anything
  • One-click keep or delete per group, or select individually
  • Export clean images directly to a folder of your choice
  • Adjustable sensitivity — strict matching or looser visual similarity
  • Supports JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, GIF, BMP and more
  • Up to 1,000 images per session

// 03 — Synthesiser & Sequencer
DP‑ONE MKVI

A standalone synthesiser and step sequencer for Mac. No plugins, no DAW required.

Built from scratch using the Web Audio API and Electron. Three synth engines, a seven-track drum sequencer, dual bass sequencer with six engine types, full filter bank, effects chain, pitch wheel, and USB MIDI keyboard support. Audio routes into Logic Pro, Ableton, or GarageBand via BlackHole. Patterns export as MIDI.

  • SuperSaw, Moog Bass, and FM Lead synth engines — 8-voice polyphony
  • 7-track drum sequencer — 8 or 16 steps
  • Dual bass sequencer — 6 engine types
  • Full filter bank — LP / HP / BP / Notch / Peak EQ / Shelf
  • Effects — Chorus, BPM-locked Echo, Convolution Reverb
  • 37-key keyboard with pitch wheel — USB MIDI support
  • Routes to Logic Pro, Ableton, GarageBand via BlackHole
  • MIDI export — drums, bass, and synth on separate channels

Sasha & John Digweed — Renaissance

// Click to enjoy

In 1994, Sasha & John Digweed changed electronic music with Renaissance: The Mix Collection — the first official nightclub "sampler."

The album was a masterclass in early 90s progressive house and trance. It wasn't just a hit — it was a cultural milestone.

From its iconic Sistine Chapel artwork to the triple-CD package, it defined the sound of a generation.

WIDELY REGARDED AS THE "HOLY GRAIL" OF DJ MIX COMPILATIONS, RENAISSANCE: THE MIX COLLECTION IS THE 1994 DEBUT MASTERPIECE FROM BRITISH ICONS SASHA & JOHN DIGWEED.

IT DIDN'T JUST CAPTURE A MOMENT IN TIME; IT REDEFINED THE ELECTRONIC MUSIC INDUSTRY BY PROVING THAT A DJ MIX COULD BE A HIGH-ART, COMMERCIAL POWERHOUSE.

THE DEFINITIVE SOUND OF AN ERA

RELEASED ON 14 OCTOBER 1994, THIS TRIPLE-CD SET SERVED AS A SONIC BLUEPRINT FOR THE LEGENDARY RENAISSANCE NIGHTCLUB.

IT SEAMLESSLY WEAVES TOGETHER HOUSE, PROGRESSIVE HOUSE, AND EARLY TRANCE, FEATURING TRACKS FROM TITANS LIKE LEFTFIELD, UNDERWORLD, AND AGE OF LOVE.

ARTISTIC PRESENTATION

HOUSED IN AN ICONIC TRIPLE DIGIPACK, THE ARTWORK FEATURES MICHELANGELO’S PAINTING OF THE PROPHET ZECHARIAH FROM THE SISTINE CHAPEL, SIGNALING THE ALBUM'S AMBITIOUS, "HIGH-CULTURE" INTENT.

THE MIX

THE THREE DISCS (BLUE, ORANGE, AND GREEN) ARE CELEBRATED FOR THEIR "EFFORTLESS" AND "HEDONISTIC" FLOW, CAPTURING THE TRANSITION OF DANCE MUSIC FROM UNDERGROUND RAVES TO A GLOBAL AUDIENCE.

LEGACY AND CRITICAL ACCLAIM

THE ALBUM'S IMPACT WAS IMMEDIATE AND ENDURING, EARNING A SILVER CERTIFICATION IN THE UK AND CEMENTING SASHA AND DIGWEED AS THE PREMIER ARCHITECTS OF THE "PROGRESSIVE" SOUND.

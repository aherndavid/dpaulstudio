// How to Use

1
Load ImagesDrop a folder or select files. All images are loaded locally — nothing is uploaded.
2
Scan for DuplicatesAdjust the threshold (higher = looser matching) then hit Scan. Groups of similar images appear.
3
Review Duplicate GroupsUse KEEP ALL or DEL DUPS per group, or click individual cards to toggle keep ✓ / delete ✕.
4
Manual SelectSwitch to Manual Select in the sidebar to browse all loaded images and click any image to mark it for deletion — regardless of duplicates.
5
ExportHit Export Kept in the sidebar to download all non-deleted images. Files download one at a time to avoid browser limits.
⚠ No files are ever deleted from disk — only from this session's export.
⚠ Exports go to your Downloads folder one at a time (300ms stagger).

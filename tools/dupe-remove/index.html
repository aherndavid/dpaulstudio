Perceptual Hashing Uses dHash to compare visual structure — not bytes. Catches duplicates across different compressions, sizes and export settings.
Group Review Similar images cluster automatically. Review each group with bulk Keep All or Delete Dups, or fine-tune image by image.
Drag to Compare Drag any two images into the compare panel to see them side by side at full resolution before deciding.
Adjustable Sensitivity Slide the threshold to control how aggressive matching is — from exact duplicates to visually similar shots from the same scene.
Prefix Export Export your clean set with an optional filename prefix. Staggered downloads prevent browser throttling so every file gets through.
Zero Data Retention Runs entirely in your browser. No server, no account, no analytics. Your images never leave your device.

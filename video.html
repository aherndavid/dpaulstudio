// 02 — Dream Three

What's your dream three?

A personal project in the works — Carbonography. The concept is simple: you pick your dream three vehicles, and we put them on merch. Hoodies, t-shirts, caps — built around the machines that matter to you.

This short promo was made to capture the idea. Three vehicles, fading in one by one, scored to music. The R34 Skyline GT-R, a classic Datsun, and an MV Agusta F4 RR. Not a random selection — a statement of taste. These happen to be my son Logan's dream three.

Carbonography launches later this summer. When it does, you'll be able to choose any three vehicles, configure your colourway, and order your own custom piece.
